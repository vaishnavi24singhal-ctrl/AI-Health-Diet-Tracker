# AI-Health-Diet-Tracker
AI-powered application for tracking health, diet, and fitness.
Opportunities
a. How is it different from existing ideas?

Existing apps provide generic diet plans
No focus on personalized AI-based nutrition
Limited analysis of lifestyle & health data
Most apps do not support Indian food habits
Our solution is different because:
Uses AI to personalize diet plans
Adapts to user lifestyle & goals
Focuses on Indian diet patterns
Provides health risk insights, not just tracking
. How will it solve the problem?

Collects user health & lifestyle data
Analyzes food intake using AI
Recommends custom diet plans
Provides alerts for unhealthy habits
Encourages healthier lifestyle decisions

Result:
 Better nutrition, improved health awareness, and 
prevention of lifestyle diseases.

Features
- User profile and health goals
- Daily food and calorie tracking
- BMI and health score calculation
- AI-based personalized diet recommendations
- Water intake and activity tracking
- Weekly health reports
- Notifications and alerts.
